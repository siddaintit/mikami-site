About Mikami

Mikami is a multipurpose Discord bot designed for communities that want complete server control combined with entertainment. Built for high performance and low latency, Mikami handles everything from rule enforcement to casino-style gambling.

With a full economy system, members can claim daily points, compete on leaderboards, and gamble through slots, blackjack, coinflip, roulette, and mines — all balanced and competitive.

As a moderation powerhouse, Mikami gives admins bulk message deletion, timeout controls, force-nickname management, and deep permission-based command access — keeping order without lifting a finger.

From trivia battles and waifu image commands to catbox archiving and image search — Mikami's fun commands keep the server alive and members hooked.
